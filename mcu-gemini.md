# Video Production Plan: "What If... Doomsday"

## Concept & Direction
**Logline:** In an alternate 2012, Tony Stark makes the ultimate sacrifice play, but the cosmic energy of the wormhole doesn't kill him—it corrupts him, returning him to Earth not as a hero, but as an iron-fisted dictator in a dystopian, noir-soaked New York.
**Aesthetic:** Cinematic Noir, Chiaroscuro lighting, Heavy shadows, Gritty textures, Unrelenting rain, Moody and dark atmosphere.
**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Audio/Voiceover:** Opens with distorted, echoing audio of Steve Rogers from The Avengers (2012): *"You're not the guy to make the sacrifice play."* Followed by a slow, oppressive synth-brass beat mixed with heavy rain Foley.
