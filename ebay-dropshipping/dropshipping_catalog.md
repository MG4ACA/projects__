## 📈 SEO Strategy Notes (eBay 2026)

1. **Cassini algorithm** rewards: click-through rate, sell-through rate, and positive feedback velocity
2. **Title formula:** `[Core Product] + [Feature 1] + [Feature 2] + [Use Case] + [Audience/Activity]` — max 80 chars
3. **Do NOT keyword stuff** in title — eBay penalizes it in 2025/2026
4. **Item Specifics** are critical — fill in every field (brand: Unbranded, MPN: Does Not Apply, etc.)
5. **Free shipping** on all listings — bake shipping cost into sell price
6. **30-day return policy** — improves Cassini ranking even for new sellers
7. **Use 8–10 high-quality photos** — request from CJ's photography service if needed
